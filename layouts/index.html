Messaging

We believe there shouldn't be a border wall. People should be able messaging between Apps with no trouble.

Security

State-of-the-art end-to-end encryption powered by Olm keeps your communications secure. Wormhole's encryption is based on the Double Ratchet Algorithm popularised by Signal and implemented by Matrix.org

Open-Source, Open-Standard

Wormhole open standard defines a set of open APIs for cross App communication. We also provide open source implementation that allows you to host your own, and of course you can always connect to our server.

Privacy

User and Your Data are our top priority. Wormhole is designed NOT able to see the user's message (messages are encrypted) and won't store private data.
